Getting There

AT&T Stadium is in Arlington — between Dallas and Fort Worth. No direct rail. Here's exactly how to get there without losing your mind.

Recommended Route
Step by Step
Key fact: Arlington has no light rail or subway. Every visitor needs to plan transport carefully — match-day traffic on I-30 can add 60–90 min each way.
01
TRE Rail to CentrePort

From downtown Dallas (Union Station) or Fort Worth, take the Trinity Railway Express to CentrePort/DFW Airport station. Service every 30 min on match days.

⚡ ~35 min from Dallas Union Station · $5 one-way

02
Free FIFA Shuttle

Dedicated FIFA shuttles run from CentrePort directly to AT&T Stadium gates — 10–15 min ride. Runs from 3 hrs before kickoff through 1.5 hrs post-match.

⚡ Free with match ticket · Leave 2+ hours before kickoff

03
Gate Entry

Show your mobile ticket at the gate. Clear bags only (12"×6"×12" max). Gates open 2.5 hours before kickoff. Security peaks at 90 min before.

⚡ Screenshot your ticket before entering — cellular unreliable inside

04
After the Match

Return shuttles run until the crowd clears — expect 30–45 min post-match waits. Alternatively, walk to nearby Arlington bars to wait it out, then Uber back.

⚡ Pre-set your Uber pickup point before you enter the stadium
