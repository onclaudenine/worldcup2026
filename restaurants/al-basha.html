About

Al Basha is widely regarded as one of the best halal Mediterranean restaurants in the Dallas area. Everything is made in-house — the hummus is silky, the shawarma is carved fresh, and the mixed grill platter is a feast. Richardson has one of the largest Middle Eastern communities in Texas, and Al Basha is its crown jewel. An absolute must for World Cup visitors looking for halal options.
