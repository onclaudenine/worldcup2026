About
The Details

Oak Cliff is where Dallas locals eat when they don't want to perform for tourists. The taco scene here is world-class. Torchy's got famous here. Revolver Taco was born here. And the neighborhood taquerias — cash only, no English menus, always a line — are what Dallas food writers dream about.
