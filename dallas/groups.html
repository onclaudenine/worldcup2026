Travelling Together

Group Travel Guide

Travelling with a supporters club or a group of 10+? Groups have different logistics — hotel blocks, group transport, ticket coordination, and finding each other in a 105,000-seat stadium. Here's everything you need.

Group Accommodation

Staying Together as a Group

Hotel room blocks
For groups of 10+ rooms, contact hotels directly to enquire about group rates and room blocks. Marriott, Hilton, and Hyatt all have group sales teams. Book well in advance — room blocks during the World Cup are competitive. Ask for a dedicated group contact and a rate hold.
VRBO for large groups
A large VRBO house fits 8–16 people under one roof for significantly less per person than hotel rooms. Uptown Dallas and Las Colinas have excellent options with pools. Book through vrbo.com — search by number of guests. Ideal for supporter groups travelling together.
Spread across nearby cities
Large supporters clubs often split accommodation across Dallas, Arlington, and Fort Worth. This distributes booking pressure and gives sub-groups their own base. Coordinate a central meeting point (Deep Ellum or the Fan Festival) for match days.

Getting to the Stadium

Group Transport to AT&T Stadium

TRE Train — best for groups
The Trinity Railway Express runs from downtown Dallas and Fort Worth directly to CentrePort/DFW station on match days, where FIFA provides free shuttles to the stadium. Cheap ($2.50), reliable, and the whole group rides together. Buy GoPass tickets in advance — they sell out on big match days.
Charter coach
For groups of 40+, chartering a coach from downtown Dallas to the stadium is worth considering. Several Dallas charter bus companies operate. Book in advance and agree on pickup times, including post-match. Costs roughly $400–800 for a round trip.
Rideshare convoy
For groups of 8–20, book multiple Ubers at the same time to the same location. Set a designated pickup point in advance (Uber struggles with large groups at the same address). Have everyone request simultaneously 30 minutes before planned departure.
Parking carpool
Groups with rental cars can carpool to reduce parking costs. Official AT&T Stadium parking is $30–60 per vehicle. Book parking passes in advance through the stadium website — lots fill up 2 hours before kickoff on big match days.

Finding Each Other

Meeting Points & Communication

Before the match
Designate a meeting point outside AT&T Stadium before you enter — once inside, mobile signal is poor and the stadium is enormous. Agree on Gate A, B, C etc. as your rally point.
WhatsApp group with all travellers. Share hotel addresses, ticket info, and match day plans here. Download offline maps for the Dallas area.
US SIM or eSIM — everyone in the group needs a US number with data. US Mobile eSIM is the easiest option — activate before you fly.
Best group meetup spots
The Rustic, Uptown — massive outdoor venue, handles large groups well. Great pre-match atmosphere with big screens.
Klyde Warren Park — free outdoor park in downtown Dallas. Large open space, food trucks, easy for a group to gather.
FIFA Fan Festival — the natural gathering point for supporters of all nations. Announce your group's location on national supporters club social media.

Supporters Clubs

Connect with Your Nation's Fans

Most national team supporters clubs organise group travel packages, ticket ballots, and meetup events during major tournaments. Connect with yours before you travel.

Oranje (Netherlands)
The Dutch national supporters club is one of the most organised in world football — famous orange marches to stadiums. Check oranjesupporter.nl for Dallas events.
La Albiceleste (Argentina)
Argentine supporters are expected in huge numbers as defending champions. Dallas has an established Argentine community. Social media groups organise pre-match asados.
Torcida (Brazil)
Brazilian fans bring some of the tournament's best atmosphere. Large Brazilian community in DFW area. Check official Torcida Brasil channels for organised events in Dallas.
American Outlaws (USA)
The US national team supporters club has active chapters in Dallas-Fort Worth. americanoutlaws.com — find the DFW chapter for local meetup events and pre-match gatherings.
