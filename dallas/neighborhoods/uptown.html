About

The Details

Uptown is where Dallas dresses up. McKinney Avenue is lined with patio restaurants, wine bars, and rooftop lounges. The M-Line Trolley runs the length of the strip for free. It's the best neighborhood for pre-match dining and post-match cocktails in equal measure.
