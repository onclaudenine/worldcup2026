🏛️ Arts District

The largest contiguous arts district in the US. Home to the Nasher Sculpture Center, Dallas Museum of Art, and the Meyerson Symphony Center.

culture DART accessible

About

The Details

The Dallas Arts District spans 68 acres and contains more LEED-certified buildings than any other arts district in the world. The Dallas Museum of Art is free every day. The Nasher Sculpture Center has one of the finest sculpture gardens in North America.
