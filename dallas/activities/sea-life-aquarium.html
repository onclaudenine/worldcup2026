🦈 SEA LIFE Grapevine Aquarium

Walk-through ocean tunnel aquarium with sharks, rays, seahorses, and a touch pool. Right next to LEGOLAND — combine both for a full family day.

Indoor Attraction 📍 Grapevine (near DFW Airport) 👨‍👩‍👧 All ages 🚗 Car Recommended

Full Details

Why Visit

SEA LIFE Grapevine is a compact but beautifully designed aquarium with an impressive 360° ocean tunnel where sharks and rays swim overhead. The rockpool touch tank lets kids handle starfish and crabs. The jellyfish display is genuinely mesmerizing for all ages. It's connected to Grapevine Mills Mall and directly next door to LEGOLAND Discovery Center — buying combo tickets for both saves money and makes for a perfect full-day outing.
