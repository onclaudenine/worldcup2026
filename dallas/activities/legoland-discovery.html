🧱 LEGOLAND Discovery Center

Fully indoor LEGO world with 4D cinema, rides, building zones, and a miniature LEGO Dallas cityscape. Perfect for ages 3–12 on a hot Texas afternoon.

Indoor Attraction 📍 Grapevine (near DFW Airport) 👨‍👩‍👧 3–12 years 🚗 Car Recommended

Full Details

Why Visit

LEGOLAND Discovery Center Grapevine is the ultimate rainy-day or heat-escape for younger kids. The MINILAND section has an impressive LEGO replica of Dallas including AT&T Stadium — great for World Cup families. The factory tour teaches how LEGO bricks are made. Two rides (a laser-blasting dark ride and a merlin's apprentice flying ride), two 4D films, and unlimited building zones. Adults must be accompanied by a child.
