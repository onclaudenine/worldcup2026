Indoor Shooting Range

One of the most uniquely American experiences available to international visitors. A professional indoor gun range with full instruction, safety equipment, and a wide range of firearms to try — no prior experience needed.

For visitors from countries where civilian firearms are restricted or banned, shooting at an American gun range is a genuinely memorable experience. Shoot Smart is one of DFW's most professional and welcoming ranges — staff provide thorough safety briefings and are experienced with first-time shooters from around the world. All safety equipment is included. An air-conditioned indoor range makes this a good option during the peak summer heat of the World Cup.

What's Included

The Experience

Safety Briefing
Every visitor receives a full safety briefing before entering the range. Range Safety Officers are on duty at all times. This is non-negotiable and thorough — you will feel confident before picking up a firearm.
Firearms to Choose From
Pistols, revolvers, semi-automatic rifles (.22 to .45), and shotguns all available to rent. Staff will recommend the right option for a first-timer — typically a .22 rifle or 9mm handgun to start.
Safety Equipment
Ear protection and safety glasses provided and included in the range fee. Closed-toe shoes are required. No open-toed shoes or sandals permitted on the range floor.
Your Target
Paper targets are included. At the end of your session, you can take your target home as a souvenir. A surprisingly popular memento from the uniquely American experience.

Important Notes

Before You Book

Passport required. Non-US visitors must present a valid passport to rent firearms. Your driving licence from your home country is not sufficient on its own.
Book a lane in advance. Shoot Smart gets busy on weekends — lanes sell out. Book online at shootsmart.com or call ahead. Walk-ins are accepted when lanes are available.
Closed-toe shoes only. This is a strict safety rule — no exceptions. Wear trainers or any closed shoe. Sandals, flip-flops, or open-toed shoes are not permitted.
Age restrictions. Must be 21+ to rent handguns without a guardian present. 18+ for rifles and shotguns. Valid photo ID required for all shooters.
