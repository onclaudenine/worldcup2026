🌸 Dallas Arboretum & Botanical Garden

Stunning 66-acre garden on White Rock Lake with a dedicated children's adventure garden, splash areas, and some of the most beautiful landscaping in Texas.

Outdoor Activity 📍 East Dallas (White Rock Lake) 👨‍👩‍👧 All ages 🚆 DART Accessible

Full Details

Why Visit

The Dallas Arboretum is 66 acres of extraordinary gardens on the shores of White Rock Lake. The Rory Meyers Children's Adventure Garden is one of the finest children's garden spaces in America — 8 acres of interactive science and nature exhibits designed specifically for kids, including a splash zone that's essential in summer. The views across the lake to the Dallas skyline are stunning. Visit in the morning — it gets very hot by afternoon.
