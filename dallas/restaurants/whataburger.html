Whataburger is not just a burger chain — it is a Texas religion. Founded in Corpus Christi in 1950 and now a DFW staple, the orange-and-white striped restaurants are open 24 hours. The Whataburger with jalapeños, a side of seasoned fries, and a Dr Pepper is as Texan as it gets. International visitors consistently rank it as a highlight of their Texas food experience.
