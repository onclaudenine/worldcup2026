Raising Cane's was founded in Baton Rouge but has become a Texas institution. The menu has exactly one thing: chicken tenders. Always fresh, always crispy, always served with the secret Cane's sauce. Multiple DFW locations including near AT&T Stadium. Open very late — great after a match.
