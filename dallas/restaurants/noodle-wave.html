About

Noodle Wave has been the go-to halal Thai restaurant in the Dallas area since 2003, and for good reason. Chef Rosie's recipes span traditional Thai to fusion — pad Thai, penang curry, almond sriracha chicken, cashew udon noodles, and freshly fried spring rolls. With 5 locations across DFW including Richardson and Irving, it's the most accessible halal option for World Cup visitors. The Richardson location is closest to downtown Dallas via DART.
