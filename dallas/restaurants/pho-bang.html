Pho Bang

The best pho in DFW — a 30-year Vietnamese institution with enormous bowls, authentic broth, and bánh mì.

Pho Bang has been serving DFW's Vietnamese community for over 30 years. The pho broth is simmered from beef bones for hours — clear, rich, and deeply fragrant. Portions are enormous. This is a cash-preferred, no-frills dining room in Dallas's Vietnamese district. Worth the drive for the authentic experience.

Insider Tips

→Pho tai (rare beef) is the classic order
→The bánh mì are excellent and under $5

→Vietnamese iced coffee (cà phê sữa đá) is mandatory
