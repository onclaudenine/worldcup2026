Ramen Suzuki does one thing perfectly. The tonkotsu broth is simmered for 18 hours, the noodles are made fresh daily, and the portions are enormous. It's in a strip mall in Addison, 20 minutes from downtown Dallas, and worth every minute of the drive. Japanese expats rate it as authentic. Weekend waits can hit 45 minutes — arrive early or on a weekday.
