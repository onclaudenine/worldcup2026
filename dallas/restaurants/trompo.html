Trompo

The best al pastor tacos in Dallas. Slow-roasted pork on a vertical spit, hand-cut to order onto fresh corn tortillas. Cash only.

Trompo does one thing — al pastor tacos — and does it better than anyone in Dallas. The pork shoulder is marinated and slow-roasted on a traditional vertical trompo spit, shaved to order onto fresh corn tortillas with pineapple and cilantro. The line is always long. It is always worth it.
