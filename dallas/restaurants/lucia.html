Why Go

Lucia is one of the most acclaimed restaurants in Dallas, full stop. Chef David Uygur sources everything locally and makes pasta daily. The menu changes weekly. It seats 36 people. During the World Cup, getting a reservation will be nearly impossible — try OpenTable at midnight exactly 30 days before your date.
