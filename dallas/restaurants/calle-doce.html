Calle Doce

Family-run Mexican seafood restaurant since 1981 in a charming Oak Cliff home. Veracruz-style shrimp and grilled fish.

The Sanchez family has been serving Oak Cliff from this charming converted home since 1981. Calle Doce leans into Veracruz-style seafood. The Camaron a la Veracruzana is the signature dish. Warm family atmosphere that feels nothing like a tourist restaurant.
