In-N-Out Burger has a cult following worldwide. The patties are never frozen, the buns are baked fresh daily, and the secret menu (Animal Style — mustard-cooked patty, pickles, grilled onions) is worth knowing. Despite the global fame, prices stay incredibly low. DFW has multiple locations. The line is usually worth the wait.
