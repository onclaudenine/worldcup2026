Monkey King Noodle brings Xi'an-style Chinese street food to Deep Ellum. The noodles are hand-pulled to order — a short wait is normal and worth it. The cumin lamb with hand-pulled noodles is the signature dish. Vegetarian options are extensive. A great cheap lunch before exploring Deep Ellum's music and food scene.
