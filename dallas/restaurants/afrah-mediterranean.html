About

Afrah is a Richardson institution — a sprawling halal Mediterranean restaurant with a full bakery attached. The menu covers Lebanese, Syrian, and Palestinian dishes with exceptional depth. The fattoush salad is the best in Dallas, the kafta kebabs are charcoal-grilled, and the pastry case is loaded with fresh baklava, knafeh, and maamoul. For World Cup visitors from the Middle East, this is the closest thing to home.
