Javier's Gourmet Mexicano

The most authentic upscale Mexican restaurant in Dallas. Old Mexico City elegance, Filete Cantinflas, and a wine list that rivals fine dining.

Javier's is the restaurant Dallas reaches for when it wants to impress. The interior evokes classic Mexico City with dark wood, candlelight, and impeccable service. The menu stays true to Mexican heritage — not Tex-Mex — with dishes like Filete Cantinflas and Mayan Style Pork. The wine cellar is one of the best in Dallas.
